About us!
Our Humble Beginnings...

Our Origin Story

Our journey began with a simple idea - to organize a one-time event where families could escape their busy routines and bask in the delight of wholesome activities. As the team brainstormed and planned, our vision grew into something much greater. We envisioned an annual tradition, a celebration that would become a beacon of happiness for generations to come.
Throughout the day, the Eid Festival team worked tirelessly to ensure everything ran smoothly. The success of the day was just the beginning. Inspired by the positive impact we had on our city, we vowed to continue their mission of spreading joy and unity. To become a beacon of hope for their community, inspiring others to come together, celebrate their diversity, and create a city where every family felt loved and cherished.

The Core Team

Behind every success story is a team of dedicated individuals. United by our faith and the spirit of Eid, we embarked on a mission to create an event that would not only commemorate this sacred occasion but also foster a sense of belonging and togetherness among everyone who calls this diverse community home. We are a group of 10 who volunteer our time and efforts with the same goal in mind, an event for EVERYONE.

Our Goals

We aim to cater to everyone's needs and wants, however impossible that may seem. Innovation fuels our creativity, pushing us to embrace new challenges and explore uncharted territories. Most importantly, our community are at the heart of everything we do, listening to their needs and implementing new ideas and tweaks is our utmost priority.
